About ABCD Dance Studio

Welcome to ABCD Dance Studio, where passion meets performance! Founded in 2016, ABCD Dance Studio has become a vibrant hub for dance enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Our mission is to inspire and nurture the love for dance in a fun, supportive, and professional environment.

At ABCD Dance Studio, we believe that dance is more than just movement; it’s a way to express yourself, connect with others, and experience the joy of creativity. Join us and be a part of our dance family. Whether you’re looking to prepare for a special event, pick up a new hobby, or pursue a professional dance career, ABCD Dance Studio is here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Our Offerings:

  • Sangeet & Wedding Events: Make your special day unforgettable with our personalized Sangeet and wedding dance choreography. Our experienced instructors will help you create beautiful, memorable performances for your celebrations.
  • Dance Events: Whether it's a corporate event, birthday party, or any special occasion, we bring the energy and excitement with our dynamic dance performances. Let us add a spark to your event with customized dance routines.
  • DJ Events: Our DJ services are designed to keep the party going all night long. With a mix of the latest hits and timeless classics, our DJs will ensure your event is a memorable one.
  • Regular Classes: From contemporary to classical, hip-hop to Bollywood, our regular dance classes cater to a variety of styles. Our curriculum is designed to help you learn and grow as a dancer, whether you’re a beginner or an advanced performer.
  • Kids Batch: Our children’s dance classes are crafted to be fun and educational. We focus on building coordination, confidence, and creativity in a positive and engaging atmosphere.
  • Adult Batch: It’s never too late to start dancing! Our adult dance classes are perfect for those looking to learn new skills, stay fit, and meet like-minded dance enthusiasts.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Instructors: Our team of professional and passionate instructors brings a wealth of experience and a commitment to helping each student reach their full potential.
  • Community Focus: We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone can share their love for dance.
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: Our studio is equipped with modern amenities and a spacious dance floor, providing the perfect environment for learning and practicing.
  • Performance Opportunities: We offer numerous opportunities for our students to showcase their talents through performances, competitions, and community events.
